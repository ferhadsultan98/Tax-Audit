About the Journal

üéØ Mission

The mission of the Shusha Scientific Journal (SSJ) is to empower scientific inquiry and innovation by providing an inclusive, ethical, and globally recognized platform for researchers, educators, and practitioners. We are committed to nurturing knowledge that drives positive social change, technological advancement, and environmental sustainability‚Äîstarting from the heart of Shusha, Azerbaijan, to the world.

üåü Vision

To become a globally respected interdisciplinary journal that bridges regional knowledge with international standards, promotes ethical research, and contributes to shaping a smarter, more just, and sustainable future through science and education.

üìå Aims & Scope

Shusha Scientific Journal is dedicated to publishing high-impact, peer-reviewed, and ethically sound research that addresses critical global and regional challenges. Inspired by Shusha's rich cultural heritage, intellectual tradition, and resilience, the journal fosters a scholarly ecosystem where science, society, and innovation converge.
